FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do my photos get uploaded anywhere?+

No. Everything runs locally in your browser — your photos never leave your device, on Aegis or Eclipse.

What exactly does Xyris remove?+

GPS coordinates, camera make & model, the capture date & time, and software / OS tags embedded in the file. In the Pro Plan, it can also help you find further vulnerabilities in your photo which potential hackers can exploit to breach your information. Xyris Vision prevents this by giving you an option to modify certain parts of the image so that your data stays safe.

Will cleaning change how my photo looks?+

No. The pixels stay the same — Xyris just re-saves the image without the hidden data attached.

Which file types are supported?+

Any common image format — JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP and more. Your cleaned copy is re-encoded in the same format wherever your browser supports it.

Is my original file modified?+

Never. You download a clean copy; your original stays exactly as it was.
